AI tools handle the technical work (writing, image creation, voiceover, video editing) that previously required years of skill. This can generate income within days. YouTube automation and digital product sales take longer to ramp up but have higher long-term earning potential.

    • Beginner (0–3 months): $200–$1,000/month from freelancing or small digital product sales

    YouTube automation means running a faceless YouTube channel where AI handles most of the production work. The workflow: use AI to research trending topics and write scripts, use a text-to-speech or AI voice tool for narration, use AI video or stock footage tools for visuals, then upload. No filming yourself required. Successful channels monetise through AdSense, affiliate links, and sponsorships. Top channels in this space earn $5,000–$50,000/month.

    • Etsy — AI art prints, wall art, planners, templates (highest traffic for beginners)
    • Gumroad / Payhip — prompt packs, AI guides, ebooks, templates
    • Amazon KDP — AI-assisted low-content books, journals, activity books
    • Creative Market — design assets, fonts, UI kits created with AI assistance

    Start with one platform, validate demand, then expand. Etsy is the easiest entry point for most beginners.
