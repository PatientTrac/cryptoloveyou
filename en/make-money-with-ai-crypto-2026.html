Frequently Asked Questions

Staking Bittensor (TAO) subnets offers among the highest yields for active participants, while liquidity provision on AI token pools (TAO/ETH, RENDER/USDC) generates passive fees. Running a Grass or io.net node requires initial hardware but generates ongoing rewards. The best method depends on your capital, technical ability, and risk tolerance.

TAO staking involves delegating to subnet validators on the Bittensor network. You'll need TAO tokens, a compatible wallet (Talisman or the official Bittensor CLI), and to select a reputable validator. Annual yields vary by subnet activity — some subnets have generated 20–40% APY, though this fluctuates significantly with network conditions and token price.

Render Network allows GPU owners to rent their processing power to creators needing rendering or AI compute. To earn RENDER, you need a compatible NVIDIA GPU, download the RNDR client, and connect to the network. Earnings depend on GPU quality, job availability, and the current RENDER token price. High-end gaming GPUs (RTX 3080+) earn the most.

AI crypto nodes are computers that contribute to decentralised AI networks — providing bandwidth, compute, or data storage. Projects like Grass, io.net, and Nosana run node programs where participants earn tokens. Earnings vary widely: Grass browser nodes earn modest passive income, while io.net GPU nodes can generate more significant returns. Always research each project's tokenomics before investing in hardware.

AI copy trading lets you automatically mirror the trades of top-performing traders, using AI to select and manage the copying. Bybit, OKX, and MEXC all offer AI copy trading features. While it can generate returns, it carries significant risks — past performance doesn't guarantee future results, and you are exposed to the same volatility as the lead trader. Use only money you can afford to lose.

AI project airdrops typically reward early users, testnet participants, and community contributors. To maximise airdrop eligibility: use the protocol's testnet, hold or stake the native token, complete on-chain tasks (bridge, swap, provide liquidity), follow official channels, and join Discord/Telegram. Be aware that airdrop farming requires time, small gas fees, and carries no guarantee of reward.

Virtuals Protocol allows developers and non-developers to create, deploy, and monetise AI agents as on-chain assets. Revenue comes from usage fees, token launches, and secondary market sales of the agent NFT. Early adopters with well-designed, useful agents have earned significant returns. However, this space is highly speculative and requires both creativity and market timing.

AI token yield farming on DEXs (Uniswap, Aerodrome, Jupiter) can generate 10–80% APY depending on the pool, but carries significant impermanent loss risk if token prices diverge. The highest yields usually come with the highest risks. Only farm with tokens you already hold long-term and would be comfortable holding even if the yield disappeared. This is not financial advice.
